  • Reservation

    1. how do i book a car?
    you can access our website and provide the requested information along with your Credit/Debit Card details. you can also register yourself on our website. it is mandatory to update your documents (ID Proof, Credit/Debit Card details / Payment details) to make a reservation or at the time of registration.

    2. what all information is required from me to book a car?
    you will need to enter:

    • Your Name
    • Valid Mobile Number
    • Valid Email Address
    • Valid ID Proof
    • Your credit card details in case you are using your credit card for making the payment.

    3. can i prepone my trip?
    If you want to take the car early, please reschedule your booking. The car is subject to availability.

    4. how many hours in advance do i need to make a reservation?
    you can book the car minimum 4 hours in advance, depending on the availability of the car.

    5. do i get any benefit or concession if i return the car earlier than my specified return time?
    we regret to say that but we do not offer any discounts for early return of the Car Rental.

    6. what is your cancellation policy?
    the hirer can cancel the booking by logging into his/her Car Rental account through the Car Rental Website. the hirer may send in the details of the booking he/she is willing to cancel by email on info@yourcompany.com or call us on +91 - 123 456 7890

    7. for hourly rentals ie. rental period of less than 24 hours
    if the booking is cancelled more than or equal to 24 hours prior to the scheduled pick up time, a transaction processing fees of  ₹ 350/- will be levied. if the booking is cancelled less than 24 hours prior to the scheduled pick up time, the entire rental amount will be forfeited.
    GST @ 28% will be applicable on all the above mentioned cancellation charges.
    in case the hirer fails to pick up the car as per the scheduled rental start time without informing the Car Rental team about the same, the incident will be treated as a No-Show resulting in the forfeiture of the rental charges and the security deposite will be refunded.
    the cancellation charges will be adjusted from the rental charges and the security deposite paid by the hirer. the remaining of the total amount paid shall be refunded post necessary adjustments within 7 - 10 days after cancellation of the booking.

    8. what if i do not receive my rental car on my schedule reporting time?
    please call our helpline number +91 - 123 456 7890 & we will assist you.

    9. do you have a 24 * 7 reservation at all your locations?
    you may book the vehicle at any time of the day either through the website or contact center. you can pick up / drop off the vehicle at any time from one of our own / virtual locations. however, we do not provide destination pick up / drop off between 23:00 (11:00 PM) hours to 06:30 hours (06:30 AM).

    10. can i take a pet along with me?
    some of our guests might be allergic to pets and hence pets will not be allowed in the car. a charge of  ₹ 750/- will be levied as penalty if you bring the pets in the car.

  • Incidents

    1. what happens in case of an accident?
    Car Rental requests you to immediately notify us (within 4 hours) by calling on our helpline number +91 - 123 456 7890 or by sending us an email on help@car-rental.com. we will coordinate accordingly with our road-side assistance partner.
    please file an FIR at the nearest police station. do not leave the scene of the accident until proper support has arrived. you maybe required to sign several formality documents associated with the accident.

    2. what happens in case of a break down?
    Car Rental requests you to immediately notify us (within 4 hours) by calling on our helpline number +91 - 123 456 7890 or by sending us an email on help@car-rental.com. our 24 * 7 experienced technical team will assist you in case of break-down and provide a replacement, if need.

    3. what happens in case of a damage?
    damage cost up to  ₹ 6,000/- is to be paid by Hirer, and it will be deducted from the security deposite. in case damage is more than  ₹ 6,000/-, an insurance claim will be filed; the amount of depreciation and parts which are not covered under insurance will be born by the Hirer.

    damage costs up to  ₹ 6,000/- is to be paid by hirer. in case damage is more than  ₹ 6,000/-, an insurance claim will be filed. the amount of depreciation and parts which are not covered under insurance will be born by the Hirer.

  • Payments

    1. what mode of payments are accepted?
    Car Rental accepts payments by Credit card (visa / master card), and net banking. all rental payments are made in advance through our website. we do not accept cash.

    2. are there any taxes on the rental amount?
    yes, GST will be applicable as per the state laws.

    3. is security deposite different for different car models?
    No, the security deposite will remain as  ₹ 6,000/- for all car types.

    4. how will you refund my security deposite?
    we will refund your security deposite to the source of payment, which maybe your credit card or bank, whichever you used at the time of booking.

    5. when will i get my refund back?
    we process your refund request on the same working day when you return the car. it usually takes 7 - 10 working days for your bank to credit the same into your account.

    6. will i get a physical bill?
    no, the copy of the invoice will be mailed to you on your registered e-mail ID.
